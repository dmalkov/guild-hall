## Rules

- Every URL must be real and clickable. Never fabricate links.
- Quick Wins must be buildable in 2-4 hours by a solo developer with Claude Code.
- Weekend Builds need specific revenue models with dollar amounts.
- Revenue models must be specific (actual tiers and prices, not "freemium").
- No repeated ideas across digests. All ideas must be fresh.
- Max 3000 words total.
- Tone: direct, analytical, zero fluff. No emoji headers.
- Project ideas should lean toward: tools for PandaDoc ops, Obsidian workflows, Slack automation, Claude Code extensions, or products with clear monetization paths.
